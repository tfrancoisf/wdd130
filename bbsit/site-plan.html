Home page

Introduction Welcome to babysitter, my name is Tatiana Francois and I provide home babysitting services for families in the Fort-Lauderdale area. Either in need for a time off taking care of your baby, need to go to work, or go out for an occasion, I have the solution. Bring him or her to my home base babysitting. Since a young age, I have always like being around children, I play with them and take care of them. Services I will take care of your toddler with great care the same way you would. I provide a safe environment for your kiddos where they can play, take a nap, snack time, and so on. I will change diapers, clean your child, feed your child. I will read books to the baby. Helping the baby play with toys. Putting the baby down for naps. Provide transportation for the baby as needed. Ensure the baby is protected and safe at all times. I can provide CPR and first Aid if in need.
